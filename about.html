About Hello Dear

We are here for you, anonymously.

Hello Dear is a safe, anonymous, and supportive platform where university students can share concerns, receive peer support, and find helpful university resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a supportive online space where university students can talk about academic pressure, emotional stress, relationships, career uncertainty, international student life, and general campus concerns without fear of judgement.

Why We Built Hello Dear

Many students experience stress, loneliness, and uncertainty, but not everyone feels comfortable seeking help face to face. Hello Dear was created to provide a supportive environment where students can express themselves anonymously and receive encouragement from peers.

What Makes Hello Dear Different?

Our design balances privacy, peer connection, community safety, and access to practical university support.

◉

Anonymous by Design

Real names are not displayed publicly. Students use automatically generated anonymous nicknames when posting and commenting.

♡

Peer Support Focused

Students support one another through respectful comments, encouragement, shared experiences, and helpful votes.

♢

Safe Community

Reporting tools, profanity filtering, and administrator moderation help maintain respectful and responsible discussions.

▦

University Resources

Students can quickly find counselling, academic, career, and international student support services in one place.
